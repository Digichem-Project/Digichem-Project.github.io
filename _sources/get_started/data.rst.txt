Download data
________________

In addition to viewing crucial calculation data directly in the Digichem app, you can also download
the calculation results from the Server to view directly on your own machine.

.. image:: /_static/get_started/data.png

To download data from the server, we can click the **Download** button (**â‡“**) in the relevant job row:

.. image:: /_static/get_started/data_download.png

This will add the job to the download queue, which we can view on the **Downloads** page:

.. image:: /_static/get_started/data_downloads_page.png

From here we can see the download progress of the job:

.. image:: /_static/get_started/data_download_progress.png

Once the download is complete, we can click on the download box to open the folder on our local machine:

.. image:: /_static/get_started/data_folder_structure.png
