Welcome to Digichem's documentation!
====================================

.. image:: /_static/general/Header.png

Welcome to Digichem, the computational management suite.
Digichem is a program designed to manage every part of the computational chemistry pipeline.
From the submission of calculations to the generation of data, everything is handled for you, automatically.
